This collaboration between Amazon Advertising and Kinder added a virtual spin to the much loved Kinder characters and provided a joyful new style of easter egg hunt for children, via an AR easter egg hunt.

I had the pleassure to work as a modeller, animator and leading the technical side of this models, to export them to .glb files, so the AR developers could implement it with Unity.
