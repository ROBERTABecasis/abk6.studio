ABK6 is Robert Abecasis' nickname. He is a director focusing on animation, design, storytelling, direction, and creativity.

With a strong background in advertising motion design and video game ads, he directed videos for popular titles like Stumble Guys, Apple Arcade, Bloons TD6, and Monopoly Go. The client list also includes notable names like Generalitat de Catalunya, Correos, Kinder, BBC, Hitachi, and more.

Over the past few years, he collaborated with several studios, including Art&Graft, Animade, The Others, Drasik Studio, Wildthings, among others.

He has been partnering with Stumble Guys for the last two years, producing numerous videos that have surpassed 4 million views on YouTube, including titles like Smurfs, UFO, Barbie, and Acid Pool, with many others exceeding 1 million views. In these projects, we've collaborated with major IPs such as Spongebob, Mr. Beast, Rabbids, Ghostbusters, and more.

ABK6 is not a studio, but it can become one for certain projects that require it. ABK6 already has some projects entirely produced by itself and has a roster of freelancers that are usual collaborators.

If you're a brand, a game studio, or an animation studio with a project in mind, we'd love to chat. Come say hi!
